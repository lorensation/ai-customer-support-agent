# Frequently Asked Questions - SaaSify

## Account & Billing

### How do I create an account?
Visit saasify.com/signup and click "Start Free Trial". Enter your email, create a password, and verify your email address. No credit card required for the free plan.

### How do I upgrade my plan?
Go to Settings → Billing → Change Plan. Select your desired plan and enter payment information. Upgrades take effect immediately and you'll be prorated for the current billing period.

### Can I cancel my subscription anytime?
Yes, you can cancel anytime from Settings → Billing → Cancel Subscription. You'll retain access until the end of your current billing period. No refunds for partial months.

### What payment methods do you accept?
We accept all major credit cards (Visa, Mastercard, American Express, Discover), PayPal, and bank transfers for Enterprise customers.

### Do you offer discounts?
Yes! We offer:
- 20% discount for annual billing
- 30% discount for non-profits and educational institutions
- Custom pricing for Enterprise customers (100+ users)
Contact sales@saasify.com for discount codes.

## Technical Support

### How do I reset my password?
Click "Forgot Password" on the login page, enter your email, and follow the reset link sent to your inbox. The link expires after 1 hour.

### How do I invite team members?
Go to Settings → Team → Invite Members. Enter email addresses (one per line) and assign roles. Invites expire after 7 days.

### Can I integrate with other tools?
Yes! We have native integrations with:
- Slack, Microsoft Teams
- Google Workspace, Microsoft 365
- GitHub, GitLab, Bitbucket
- Zapier for 1000+ additional apps
Check Settings → Integrations for setup instructions.

### Is there a mobile app?
Yes, download from:
- iOS: App Store (requires iOS 14 or later)
- Android: Google Play (requires Android 8.0 or later)
All features available on mobile with offline sync support.

### How do I export my data?
Go to Settings → Data Export. Select format (JSON, CSV, or PDF) and click "Request Export". You'll receive a download link via email within 24 hours.

## Features & Usage

### What's the file size limit?
- Free plan: 10MB per file
- Professional plan: 100MB per file
- Enterprise plan: 2GB per file

### How many projects can I create?
- Free plan: 10 projects
- Professional plan: Unlimited
- Enterprise plan: Unlimited

### Can guests access my workspace?
Yes, you can invite unlimited guests for free on all plans. Guests can only access projects you specifically share with them.

### How does time tracking work?
Click the timer icon on any task to start tracking. The timer runs in the background and syncs across devices. View reports in Analytics → Time Tracking.

### Can I customize notifications?
Yes! Go to Settings → Notifications to choose:
- Email, in-app, or push notifications
- Notification frequency (real-time, hourly digest, daily digest)
- Which events trigger notifications
- Do Not Disturb schedule

## Security & Privacy

### Where is my data stored?
Data is stored in AWS data centers with redundancy across multiple regions. You can choose your primary region during setup.

### Is my data encrypted?
Yes, all data is encrypted:
- In transit: TLS 1.3
- At rest: AES-256 encryption
- Backups: Encrypted and replicated daily

### Who can see my data?
Only users you explicitly invite. SaaSify employees cannot access your data without permission, except for support requests or legal requirements.

### Do you sell my data?
No, never. We don't sell, rent, or share your data with third parties for marketing purposes. See our Privacy Policy for details.

### How do I enable two-factor authentication?
Go to Settings → Security → Two-Factor Authentication. Scan the QR code with Google Authenticator or Authy. Save your backup codes in a secure location.

## Troubleshooting

### Why can't I log in?
Common issues:
1. Check if Caps Lock is on
2. Clear browser cache and cookies
3. Try a different browser or incognito mode
4. Reset your password
5. Contact support if still unable to log in

### Why are my files not uploading?
Check:
1. File size doesn't exceed plan limit
2. Stable internet connection
3. Sufficient storage available
4. File format is supported
5. Browser extensions aren't blocking uploads

### Why are notifications not working?
Ensure:
1. Notifications are enabled in Settings
2. Browser notifications are allowed
3. Email notifications aren't going to spam
4. Mobile app has notification permissions

### Performance is slow. What can I do?
Try:
1. Refresh the page
2. Clear browser cache
3. Disable browser extensions
4. Check internet speed
5. Update to latest app version
6. Contact support if issue persists
